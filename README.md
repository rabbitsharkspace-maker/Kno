# Kno - The Spatial Knowledge OS

**Kno** is a minimalist, AI-powered knowledge internalization engine designed to help you capture, distill, and internalize wisdom through a spatial interface and advanced AI workflows.

![Kno App Preview](public/kno.png)

## 🚀 Vision
In an era of information overload, Kno helps you move from "Feed to Knowledge." It's not just a note-taking app; it's a dedicated environment for deep work and information processing.

## ✨ Key Features

### 📥 Intelligence Capture
- **Universal Inbox**: Upload PDFs, text documents, or paste URLs (YouTube, Articles) to instantly process content.
- **Auto-Distillation**: Every source is automatically summarized and transformed into multi-choice quizzes to test your understanding.
- **Neural Dump**: Capture voice notes or quick thoughts that the AI automatically organizes into your workspace.

### 🧠 Logic & Verification
- **🛡️ Logic Guard (Trinity Audit)**: Run deep neural audits on any source to verify factual accuracy, cognitive balance, and logical integrity.
- **Spark Matchmaking**: Automatically discover hidden connections between new information and your existing library.

### 🧪 Synthesis & Alchemy
- **Collider**: Compare multiple sources to highlight contradictions, differing perspectives, and consensus.
- **Alchemy**: Forge entirely new perspectives by synthesizing multiple concepts into a single, cohesive output.
- **Learning Paths**: Generate structured curricula from your notes to master complex topics.

### 🎨 Spatial Organization
- **Infinite Canvas**: Arrange your knowledge visually. Use groups, folders, and themes to create your mental models.
- **Smart Arrange**: Automatically tidy your workspace with grid snapping or theme-based grouping.
- **Memory Lab**: Visualize your learning progress over time with a heat map of activity and retention scores.

### 🔒 Security & Privacy
- **100% Local-First**: Your data is stored on your device, ensuring maximum privacy and offline availability.
- **BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)**: Powered by Gemini 2.0 Flash. Provide your own API key for maximum transparency and control.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
- **Frontend**: React 18, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion
- **Visuals**: D3.js (Knowledge Graphs), Lucide React (Icons)
- **AI**: Google Gemini SDK (@google/genai)
- **Core Libraries**: jsPDF, html2canvas, react-markdown

## 📥 Export Options
- **Markdown**: High-fidelity text export with Neural Audit details.
- **PDF**: Professional Insight Briefs with visual canvas previews and branded footers.
